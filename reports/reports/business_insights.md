# Retail Sales Business Insights Report

## Executive Summary

This report presents insights generated from retail transaction data analysis using Python, SQL, and Microsoft Power BI.

The objective was to identify sales trends, customer behaviour patterns, and product performance opportunities to support data-driven business decisions.

---

# Sales Performance Insights

Key observations:

- Revenue trends were analysed across different time periods.
- High-performing sales periods were identified.
- Overall business performance was evaluated using KPI metrics.

Business Impact:

Helps management understand revenue patterns and improve sales planning.

---

# Customer Insights

Analysis focused on:

- Customer purchasing behaviour
- Geographic revenue distribution
- Customer contribution patterns

Business Impact:

Supports targeted marketing strategies and customer relationship management.

---

# Product Performance Insights

Analysis identified:

- Best-performing products
- Revenue contribution
- Sales volume trends

Business Impact:

Supports inventory optimisation and product strategy decisions.

---

# Strategic Recommendations

Recommendations:

1. Prioritise high-performing products.
2. Improve inventory planning using demand patterns.
3. Focus marketing activities on high-value customers.
4. Monitor declining product categories.
5. Use analytics dashboards for continuous performance tracking.
