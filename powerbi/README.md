# Power BI Dashboard Design

## Dashboard Overview

The Retail Sales Business Intelligence Dashboard provides an interactive analytical solution to monitor sales performance, understand customer behaviour, evaluate product performance, and generate strategic business insights.

The dashboard is structured into four analytical pages:

1. Sales Overview Dashboard
2. Customer Insights
3. Product Performance
4. Strategic Insights

---

# 1. Sales Overview Dashboard

## Purpose

Provides an executive-level summary of overall business performance and key sales metrics.

## Key Metrics

- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Total Quantity Sold
- Average Order Value
- Sales Growth Trends

## Visualisations

- Revenue trend over time
- Monthly sales performance
- Sales distribution
- KPI cards
- Overall performance summary

## Business Questions Answered

- What is the overall sales performance?
- How are sales changing over time?
- Are business targets being achieved?
- What are the major revenue trends?

---

# 2. Customer Insights

## Purpose

Analyses customer purchasing behaviour and geographical sales patterns.

## Key Metrics

- Number of Customers
- Revenue Contribution
- Customer Purchase Frequency
- Country-wise Sales Performance

## Visualisations

- Customer distribution map
- Revenue by country
- Top customer segments
- Customer contribution analysis

## Business Questions Answered

- Who are the highest-value customers?
- Which regions generate the most revenue?
- Where are opportunities for market growth?

---

# 3. Product Performance

## Purpose

Evaluates product-level performance to support inventory planning and sales optimisation.

## Key Metrics

- Best-selling products
- Revenue by product
- Quantity sold
- Product contribution percentage

## Visualisations

- Top products by revenue
- Top products by quantity
- Product performance comparison
- Category/product trends

## Business Questions Answered

- Which products drive revenue?
- Which products have high demand?
- Which products require improvement?

---

# 4. Strategic Insights

## Purpose

Transforms analytical findings into actionable recommendations for business decision-makers.

## Key Insights

Examples:

- Identify high-performing products for strategic investment
- Optimise inventory based on demand patterns
- Improve customer targeting strategies
- Identify growth opportunities across regions

## Business Recommendations

- Focus marketing efforts on high-value customer segments
- Improve stock availability for high-demand products
- Monitor declining product performance
- Use historical trends for future planning

## Business Questions Answered

- What actions should management take?
- Where should resources be allocated?
- How can profitability be improved?
